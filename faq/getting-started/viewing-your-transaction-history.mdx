---
title: "Viewing Your Transaction History"
description: "You can view your transaction history on the Solana blockchain by using one of the available Solana blockchain explorers. Transactions sometimes do not show up on your Solana wallet's transaction history, so it is better to verify by using the explorer."
---

For SPL token transfers to your wallet account, sometimes these are reflected in the “Token Balance Change” tab in the associated tx link.
