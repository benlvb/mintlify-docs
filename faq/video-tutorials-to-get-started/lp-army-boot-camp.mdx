## General Instructions

Please follow the [announcement channel in the Meteora discord](https://discord.com/channels/841152225564950528/981108341482868736) for the latest instructions.

Sign up for the next session [here](https://bootcamp.meteora.ag/).

For showing up on Day 1, youâ€™ll receive the "Boot Camp Trainee" discord role. On Day 2, you will get instructions on graduation requirements. Upon successfully passing the course exam, you will receive an NFT certificate, and get promoted to the "LP Army Private" role in discord.

To graduate, trainees have to meet the following requirements. Note that these are general requirements, minor changes to the criteria are possible after each boot camp.

* Attend the boot camp for at least \~1 hour

* Add a minimum of $100 of liquidity during the given time period

* Pick any initial pair (except stables) which will test your ability to find pool opportunities

* At the end of the session, have a positive P\&L by maintaining your starting amount of tokens + have extra. Have a positive P\&L according to the copy of the [DLMM P\&L sheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1RIlAb-eilVWUVzruDUWR7Sidt8LI-GB1VIBhiKC_ccI/edit#gid=1434356868). Or view P\&L using the [community-built dashboard by AlekssRG](https://www.metlex.io/)

* Submit a task form and DM a screenshot of your P\&L and initial tx to LP instructors on discord
